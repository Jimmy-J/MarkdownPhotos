PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 2026-04-13

This Privacy Policy describes how PUSH ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address if you register by email.
  • User Content: Profile image only when you upload an avatar.
  • Usage Data: Game records and gameplay data for logged-in users.
  • Device & Connection Information: Bluetooth connection data used for device communication.
  • We do not collect photos unless you explicitly upload a profile avatar.
  • We do NOT collect location data or contacts.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Enable account features, including game records and profile data.
  • Synchronize data across devices.
  • Display profile avatars and improve user experience.

3. Camera and Photo Access

Users can set a profile avatar by taking a photo with the camera or selecting an image from the gallery. These permissions are used only for updating profile avatars.

4. Bluetooth and Location Permission

The App uses Bluetooth to connect to external devices. On some Android versions, location permission may be required for Bluetooth scanning. We do NOT collect, store, or track location data. Location permission is only used for system-required Bluetooth functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Game data, account data, and profile images may be stored on our servers.
  • We take reasonable security measures to protect user data.

6. Data Sharing

  • We do NOT sell or rent personal data.
  • We do NOT use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

7. User Rights

  • Use the App without registration.
  • Access, update, or delete account data.
  • Request data deletion or stop using the App at any time.
  • Deletion request email: info@pushpucks.com
  • Deletion request page: https://push.esface.cn/account/delete
  • We will process requests within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
